Traffic Violations & Court Case Clearance

Resolving complex traffic fines, vehicle impoundments, or court-mandated blocks (such as travel bans or assets blocks) under Abu Dhabi traffic laws and judicial systems requires specialized local knowledge. B&M provides legal representation and administrative filing services to clear files in the Judicial Department and Abu Dhabi Traffic Police.

We coordinate fine discount applications, transfer of black points, release of impounded cars, and clearing court files so businesses and residents can process their licenses and residency stamps without administrative blocks.
