Official Daman Health Insurance Processing

Having valid health insurance is a legal mandate for all UAE residents, and is a prerequisite for visa stamping, especially in Abu Dhabi under DOH regulations. B&M handles all aspects of Daman health insurance applications, renewals, and file uploads directly with the National Health Insurance Company (Daman).

We coordinate both the low-cost Abu Dhabi Basic Plan (for sponsors, domestic workers, and low-income categories) and custom enhanced corporate schemes for white-collar staff and families.
