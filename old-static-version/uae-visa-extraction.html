Document Requirements & Process

The visa extraction process varies depending on the visa category, but generally includes the following standard phases:

  1. Document Attestation: Translation and attestation of certificates by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA).
  2. Entry Permit Issuance: Application submission through ICP or GDRFA channels.
  3. Status Adjustment: Amending legal entry status within the UAE.
  4. Medical Screening & Biometrics: Coordination for mandatory health checks and Emirates ID fingerprinting.
  5. Residency Stamping: Final approval and issuance of physical/digital residency credentials.
